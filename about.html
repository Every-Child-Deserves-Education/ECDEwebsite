About Us

ECDE Foundation, or Every Child Deserves Education Foundation, is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization run by students whose goal is to contribute to the betterment of education for children worldwide by providing funds/resources to underprivileged schools. We are currently working on a project in which we create educational videos to help the underprivileged children learn about a variety of subjects.

Why choose us?

Our ability to bring outstanding people together to solve problems makes us successful
